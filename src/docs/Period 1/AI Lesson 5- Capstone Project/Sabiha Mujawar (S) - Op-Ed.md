**AI’s Impact on an Addictive Tiktok**

**You open Tiktok ‘ just for a minute’, but then suddenly before you even realize it’s 2am; here's why that keeps happening. Whether you like it or not we are all familiar with Tiktok. An addictive social media platform, where many make videos, scroll short videos, and maybe even get Tiktok famous. Now don’t get me wrong I enjoy Tiktok and think it is quite entertaining, but it is also extremely addictive. It’s not wrong to use Tiktok, but it should be used in moderation, and should never exceed to the point where it takes over our whole lives. Now why is Tiktok so addictive? The answer to that question is Tiktoks for you page AI.**

**As a social media platform Tiktok is one of the best. This platform is great for helping small creators hit it big, and is more focused on the quality of the content over the amount of followers one has. Tiktok is also a great way for people to connect, since it helps even the isolated to feel as if they have their own community. On top of that Tiktok is also useful to spread social awareness about important topics going on in our world today. It is also a great way to learn and try new things, since people are always posting new tutorials and hacks.** 

**Even though Tiktok for you page AI has an abundance of positives, it also has its fair share of negatives as well. These issues include privacy concerns, a spread of misinformation, and manipulations, but arguably the most important issue is how addictive Tiktok really is. Tiktok being addictive has led to many mental health issues, since it always leaves the brain feeling foggy or almost melted. Tiktok was designed to ensure each user maximizes their time on the platform. In order to maximize time it leads to an interference with sleep schedules, work, relationships, and responsibilities. This just makes you ask yourself, how does Tiktok grasp such a hold on you that you can never stop scrolling?**

**The reason Tiktoks for you page AI is so addictive is a result of the slot machine method. This method always makes you want more, since you never know what’s next. Each swipe could be the ‘perfect video’, which is similar to how each pull could result in even more money. Just like the slot machine that is never the case. At the end of the day we end up losing more money than we thought. Similar to how one  loses countless hours scrolling, instead of doing something productive. Tiktoks for you page creates the anticipation of dopamine, not just dopamine hits. Tiktok doesn’t limit the amount of scrolls per day, so their is never any immediate consequence. The algorithm is never ending, since there is always another video patiently waiting. This brings up the question, how can we stop this?**

**Even though these seem like unsolvable issues, there are numerous solutions. These solutions could include mandatory break reminders every 30- 60 minutes, which can not be dismissed. Daily cap times, where the app is inaccessible for a few hours, so you can’t open the app and start scrolling. Session previews could also be added to the app, which would show how many videos you’ve seen in the number of minutes you have been on the app. We do not only have to rely on Tiktok to make these changes, when we could start to make them ourselves. As a society we could start to exercise more, start getting out of the house more, and communicating with more people. This could decrease the use of social media altogether.** 

**Tiktok’s for you page AI is helpful in helping users find content they would enjoy, a great way of entertainment, and so much more. Yet we should not let this AI or any other AI completely consume us as a society. As humans we should not have to rely on AI for everything; we should learn to be more independent. Artificial intelligence is only going to evolve more and more, and it is important that we are not dependent on them. Yes, Tiktok is very addictive, but that should not stop us humans from enjoying our lives, and doing things we greatly enjoy doing.**
